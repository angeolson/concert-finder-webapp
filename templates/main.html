Concert Lineup Generator Welcome to your Concert Lineup Generator!
A few things to note:
1. The data is current as of June 22, 2023
2. You must select at least one day of the week
3. Genre/related artist information is taken from Spotify; if the artist is so underground that Spotify doesn't have this data, then you're out of luck. To see all results, leave the genre field blank.
4. If you want to search on multiple genre keywords, separate with a ':'. For example, to search for both indie and alt, type 'indie:alt' in the field.
